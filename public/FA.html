Frequently Asked Questions

How do I participate in the quiz?

To participate in a quiz, simply sign up on our website and get ready to play! Once you’ve registered, navigate to the quiz section. It’s that easy!

Are there any costs associated with taking the quizzes?

No, all quizzes on the Anytime Quizz website are completely free to participate in. They are provided as part of our commitment to promoting financial literacy and education.

What is the 'LUCKY WINNER' selection process?

From time to time, we select random lucky winners from our quiz participants. The selection is entirely random and based on predefined criteria. Winners will receive surprise gift.

How will I know if I've won a prize?

If you are selected as a lucky winner, we will announce the results daily on our WhatsApp channel. Make sure you are subscribed to our channel to stay updated. You’ll also receive a notification via the phone number you provided when participating in the quiz

What kind of prizes can I win?

Prizes vary and may include gift vouchers, financial resources, or other surprise gifts. The specific prize will be mentioned during the winner notification.

Is there any guarantee of winning a prize?

Yes, we believe that every participant is a winner. Everyone who participates in the quiz will receive a gift as a token of our appreciation. So, no matter what, you’ll walk away with something special!
